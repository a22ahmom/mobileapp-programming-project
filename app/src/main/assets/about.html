About the app

The app provides information about wild animals, more specifically top 6 animals. All animals that are presented in the app are dangerous and are a threat to humans. Information is in the form of name, location and category.

Information provided in the app concerns all those that are interested in knowing about wild animals and more specifically, hunters and those who are going on safari, to take caution for wild animals.
